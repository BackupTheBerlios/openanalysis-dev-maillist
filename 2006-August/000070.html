Hi,

You need to make following change in your  UseOA-ROSE and UseOA-Open64 scripts if
you are using OpenAnalysis r214 (onwards).

#setenv OPENANALYSIS_ARCH i686-Linux
setenv OPENANALYSIS_ARCH x86-Linux

replace the  i686-Linux Platform  with x86-Linux Platform to avoid compilation errors.
