On Apr 17, 2007, at 4:40 PM, Barbara Kreaseck wrote:

> I've added a useful new standalong routine to UseOA-Open64
> (r170) and MPICFG (r357).  It will scan a result *.out file and
> extract the dot output lines and send them to stdout.  Along
> the way, it strips out those pesky PRAGMA statements that
> Open64 inserts into the output stream during the dot output.
> Those PRAGMA inserts make the direct snarf-and-barf into
> GraphViz impossible.  No Longer!
>
> After a make in an updated UseOA-Open64, you will have an
> extractDOT executable.  Usage:
>
>   extractDOT outputFileName
>     or
>   extractDOT outputFileName > outputFileName.dot
>
> ex:  extractDOT TestResults/ICFG/functions2.b.out
>
> Enjoy!  I know I will not miss having to delete all of the
> PRAGMAs out of the middle of the statement handles at the top
> of each subroutine. =)
>
> -- Barbara
