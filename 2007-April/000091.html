Hi,

Changed the Semantics of  UnnamedRef and UnnamedLoc
to Use ExprHandle instead of StmtHandle.

UnnamedRef:

Old:
     UnnamedRef(memRefType, StmtHandle)

New:
      UnnamedRef(memRefType, ExprHandle).


UnnamedLoc:

Old:
     UnnamedLoc(StmtHandle , ....)

New:
      UnnamedLoc(ExprHandle , ....)

Corresponding modifcations are done for
the Open64IRInterface and SageIRInterface.

Regenerated TestCases on both UseOA-ROSE
and UseOA-Open64 side that are affected due
to this change.


Changes are incuded in:
OpenAnalysis Revision:  351
UseOA-Open64 Revision: 168
UseOA-Open64 Revision:  285.
